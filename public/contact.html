Frequently Asked Questions

When you pay for a job on NaijaWorks, your money goes into an escrow held by NaijaWorks — not to the worker. The worker only receives payment after you confirm the work is done and you're satisfied. This protects both parties.
Withdrawals are processed within 24–48 hours on normal business days. During bank holidays or high-volume periods it may take up to 3 business days. Minimum withdrawal is ₦10,000.
Don't confirm the job if you're not satisfied. You can raise a dispute from the contract page and our admin team will review the evidence from both sides and resolve it fairly. Your money stays in escrow until the dispute is resolved.
VIP workers pay 7% commission instead of 20%. This rate is locked when the contract is created. If your VIP expires before a job is completed, contracts created while VIP was active still use the 7% rate.

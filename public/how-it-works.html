How NaijaWorks Works

Simple, secure, and built for Nigeria. Get work done or earn money in 4 easy steps.

For Clients

Hire in 4 Simple Steps

1

Search or Post a Job

Browse workers by skill and location, or post a job describing what you need. Workers will send you offers directly.

2

Review Profiles & Negotiate

Check worker profiles, ratings, and reviews. Make an offer, negotiate the price, and agree on fair terms before paying.

3

Pay Securely via Escrow

Pay through NaijaWorks using Paystack. Your money is held in escrow — the worker can't access it until you confirm the job is done.

4

Confirm & Release Payment

Once the work is complete and you're satisfied, confirm it. Payment is instantly released to the worker. Then leave a review!

For Workers

Start Earning in 4 Steps

1

Create Your Worker Profile

Sign up as a worker, fill in your skills, set your prices, and upload portfolio samples. A strong profile gets more clients.

2

Receive & Negotiate Offers

Clients will send you job offers. Accept, decline, or counter-offer the price. You're always in control of what you accept.

3

Complete the Work

Once the client funds the contract, you'll get access to their contact details. Do the work, then mark it as done on NaijaWorks.

4

Get Paid to Your Wallet

After the client confirms, earnings land in your NaijaWorks wallet (minus commission). Withdraw to your bank account anytime.

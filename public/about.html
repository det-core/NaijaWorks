About NaijaWorks

We're on a mission to make hiring in Nigeria safe, simple, and fair for everyone.

NaijaWorks was built to solve a real problem Nigerians face every day — finding trustworthy workers and getting paid safely. Too many people have been scammed by workers who took payment and disappeared. Too many skilled workers have been exploited by clients who refused to pay after the job was done.

We built NaijaWorks to fix both of these problems at once. Clients pay into a secure escrow — money is held safely until the job is done. Workers know they'll get paid the moment the client confirms. No more trust issues. No more disputes over payment.

Our platform connects clients with verified Nigerian workers for both physical services (drivers, cleaners, plumbers, electricians, cooks) and digital/remote work (developers, designers, writers, virtual assistants). Whether you need someone in your neighborhood today or a remote professional online, NaijaWorks has you covered.
